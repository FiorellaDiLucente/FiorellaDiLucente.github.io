Intersection improvements in New Bedford (Massachusetts, US)

Info
Drainage at Acuhsnet Av.

Project duration: 18 months

BID Amount: $2 million

The work under this contract consists of:

  • pavement milling and overlay with box-widening along Acushnet Avenue from south of Cherokee Street to Tobey Street (Station 11+00 to 18+55)
  • removing and stacking or resetting of existing granite curb
  • installation of new granite curb
  • construction of sidewalks
  • hydrant removal and installation
  • waterline removal and installation
  • installation of new catch basins, manholes, tree filter boxes and drainage trunk line
  • construction of driveway aprons
  • loaming and seeding
  • installation of signs and pavement markings
  • installation of a traffic control signal system with video detection, signal posts, signal heads, mast arm assemblies, cabinet, foundation, wire and cable, pull boxes, and electrical service connection.
